About

Creams and Co. was born out of pure love and passion for ‘all things sweet’. Creating sweet confections, desserts and cakes that are both pleasing to the eye with sensational taste is what we shoot for every time with every client. We strive to bring your event/ celebration to life with your unique style and ideas. This venture that started off as a creative outlet has flourished transforming a dream into a reality.
